Grammar Notes (Gender & Number)

1. Gender (Género)

  • • Most nouns ending in -o are Masculine (el libro).
  • • Most nouns ending in -a are Feminine (la mesa).
  • • Nouns ending in -ción, -dad, -tud are almost always Feminine.
  • • Watch out! Some are irregular: el mapa, el problema, la mano.

2. Articles (Artículos)

Definite (The): El, La, Los, Las.

Indefinite (A/Some): Un, Una, Unos, Unas.

The article must match the gender and number of the noun.
