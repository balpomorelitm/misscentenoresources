Reference Notes (Gender, Articles & Number)

1. Gender (Género)

  • • Most nouns ending in -o are Masculine (el libro).
  • • Most nouns ending in -a are Feminine (la mesa).
  • • Nouns ending in -ción, -dad, -tud are almost always Feminine.
  • • Watch out! Some are irregular: el mapa, el problema, la mano.

2. Articles (Artículos)

Definite (The): Specific items. Forms: El, La, Los, Las.

Indefinite (A/Some): Non-specific items. Forms: Un, Una, Unos, Unas.

Articles must match the gender and number of the noun.

3. Number (Número)

How to form the plural in Spanish:

Ends in Vowel (a, e, i, o, u)

Add -s

Libro → Libros

Mesa → Mesas

Ends in Consonant

Add -es

Profesor → Profesores

Papel → Papeles

Ends in -z

Change -z to -ces

Actriz → Actrices

Lápiz → Lápices
