5. Grammar: Irregular Verbs

"GO" Verbs (Yo form only)

These verbs only change in the "Yo" (I) form.

  • Hacer (To do) → Hago
  • Poner (To put) → Pongo
  • Salir (To go out) → Salgo
  • Tener (To have) → Tengo
  • Decir (To say) → Digo

Stem-Changing Verbs

The vowel in the stem changes (except in nosotros/vosotros).

  • E → IE: Cerrar (Cierro), Empezar (Empiezo)
  • O → UE: Dormir (Duermo), Almorzar (Almuerzo)
  • E → I: Pedir (Pido), Servir (Sirvo)

6. Grammar: Reflexive Verbs (Rutina)

What are they?

Verbs where the action reflects back to the subject. They end in -se (e.g., Lavarse - to wash oneself).

Pronouns: Me, Te, Se, Nos, Os, Se.

Common Routine Verbs

Despertarse (e>ie) → Me despierto
Levantarse → Me levanto
Ducharse → Me ducho
Cepillarse → Me cepillo
Vestirse (e>i) → Me visto
Acostarse (o>ue) → Me acuesto
