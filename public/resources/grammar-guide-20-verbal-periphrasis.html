Unit 20: Verbal Periphrasis

Combinations of Verbs (Perífrasis Verbales)

1. Grammar: Types & Uses
2. Practice: Exercises

1. What is a Verbal Periphrasis?

It is a combination of two verbs that function as one single meaning.
Structure: Verb 1 (Conjugated) + (Link: a/de/que) + Verb 2 (Infinitive/Gerund)

With Infinitive (To do)

  • Ir a + Inf: Future / Plan.
    Voy a comer. (I am going to eat.)
  • Tener que + Inf: Obligation.
    Tengo que estudiar. (I have to study.)
  • Empezar a + Inf: Start action.
    Empiezo a leer. (I start to read.)
  • Volver a + Inf: Repetition (Again).
    Vuelvo a ver la peli. (I watch the movie again.)
  • Dejar de + Inf: Stop action.
    Dejo de fumar. (I stop smoking.)
  • Acabar de + Inf: Recent past.
    Acabo de llegar. (I have just arrived.)

With Gerund (-ing)

  • Estar + Gerundio: Ongoing action.
    Estoy comiendo. (I am eating.)
  • Seguir + Gerundio: Continuity (Still doing).
    Sigo trabajando. (I am still working / keep working.)
