1. Relative Pronouns (Pronombres Relativos)

What are they?

Relative pronouns connect sentences to give more information about a noun (person, place, or thing) mentioned before.

QUE (That / Which / Who)

The most common. Use it for people OR things.

El libro que leo es bueno.
(The book that I read...)

DONDE (Where)

Use it only for places / locations.

La casa donde vivo.
(The house where I live.)

QUIEN / QUIENES (Who)

Use it only for people, usually after a preposition (a, con, de...) or commas.

La chica con quien hablo.
(The girl with whom I speak.)
