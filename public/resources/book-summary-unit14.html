4. Grammar: Preterite vs. Imperfect

When to use which?

This is the key contrast for narrating stories or history in Spanish. Use this guide to decide:

IMPERFECTO (The Background)

  • Descriptions (Age, Weather, Emotions).
  • Habitual actions ("Used to").
  • Ongoing actions ("Was ...-ing").

Hacía sol.

INDEFINITO (The Action)

  • Finished events.
  • Specific time (Yesterday, 1945).
  • Sudden interruptions.

El conflicto empezó en 1936.
