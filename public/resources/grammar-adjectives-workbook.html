Adjetivos Posesivos

They go BEFORE the noun.

🌟 Reglas de Oro

Use mi, tu, su for singular objects. Add an "s" if the object is plural (mis libros).

Pronombres Posesivos

They REPLACE the noun. Always use el, la, los, las.

🔍 La Diferencia

Instead of saying "My house" (Mi casa), you say "The mine" (La mía). The pronoun must match the object it replaces.
