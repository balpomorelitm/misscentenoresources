Adjetivos Demostrativos

This, That, That over there...

📘 Gramática: Distancia y Concordancia

Demonstratives: In Spanish, we choose the word based on how far the object is from the speaker. They must agree in gender and number with the noun. Hover over the words to see their meaning!
Aquí Cerca Este / Esta
Ahí Distancia Media Ese / Esa
Allí / Allá Lejos Aquel / Aquella
Distancia Masc. Sing. Fem. Sing. Masc. Plural Fem. Plural
Cerca (This) este esta estos estas
Media (That) ese esa esos esas
Lejos (Over there) aquel aquella aquellos aquellas
