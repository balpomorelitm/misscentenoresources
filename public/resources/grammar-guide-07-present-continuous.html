Actions happening NOW & Writing Blogs

1. Grammar: Present Progressive
2. Text Type: Blog

1. The Present Progressive (Estar + Gerundio)

Formula:

Verb ESTAR (conjugated) + GERUND (-ing)

Example: Yo estoy hablando (I am speaking).

Step 1: Conjugate ESTAR

  • Yo estoy
  • Tú estás
  • Él/Ella está
  • Nosotros estamos
  • Ellos/Ellas están

Step 2: Form the Gerund

  • -AR verbs: Remove -ar, add -ando (Cantando)
  • -ER/-IR verbs: Remove -er/-ir, add -iendo (Comiendo, Escribiendo)

⚠️ Irregular Gerunds to Memorize:

  • Leer -> Leyendo (Reading)
  • Dormir -> Durmiendo (Sleeping)
  • Pedir -> Pidiendo (Asking for)
  • Ir -> Yendo (Going)

Text Type: The Blog

A blog is a personal online diary or informational site. It's updated regularly and often allows readers to comment.

Key Features (Características):

  • Header: Blog name, post title, date, time, author.
  • Tone: Usually informal and personal ("Yo", opinions).
  • Structure: Introduction, Body paragraphs, Conclusion/Call to action.
  • Interaction: "Comments" section at the end.
